Our Story

More than
a coffee shop

Coffee & Co. was born from a single, unshakeable belief: that great coffee deserves an equally great space. Nestled on Sunland Boulevard, we have become a neighborhood institution — a warm, unhurried corner of the world where strangers become regulars and regulars become friends.

We source our beans from small, ethical farms, roast in small batches, and brew each cup with deliberate care. Whether it's your first visit or your five hundredth, we pour the same intention into every glass.
